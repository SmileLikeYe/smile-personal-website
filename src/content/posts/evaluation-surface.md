MTEB work taught me that evaluation is not a backend-only concern. A benchmark becomes useful when people can understand what changed, compare models cleanly, and trust the leaderboard.

The product questions are practical:

- Can a contributor understand why a result changed?
- Can a reader compare models without reading hidden assumptions?
- Can the UI make benchmark complexity legible?

That is why evaluation belongs in my model diagram. It is the loop that keeps output honest.
